🎓 PostWach: Your AI Research Assistant

PostWach is an AI-powered research co-pilot designed for academic researchers. It combines multiple specialized AI agents that work together to help with different research tasks.

Key Capabilities:

  • Literature Review: Automatically finds, reads, and summarizes academic papers
  • Writing Assistance: Helps draft papers, respond to reviewers, and document research
  • Formal Methods: Constructs mathematical proofs and verifies system models
  • Project Management: Tracks tasks, workflows, and research progress

Why "PostWach"?

The name is a play on words: Post (postdoctoral research) + Wach (Dr. Wach's last name, pronounced like "Doc"). It's a personalized research co-pilot tailored for systems engineering and formal methods research.
