# Reference List: Agentic AI Swarms for Systems Engineering

Compiled for academic paper on agentic AI swarms with systems engineering process applications.

---

## 1. Foundational Multi-Agent Systems

### Core Texts

[1] M. Wooldridge, *An Introduction to MultiAgent Systems*, 2nd ed. Chichester, UK: Wiley, 2009.
- Definitive textbook on MAS fundamentals, agent architectures, coordination

[2] G. Weiss, Ed., *Multiagent Systems: A Modern Approach to Distributed Artificial Intelligence*. Cambridge, MA: MIT Press, 1999.
- Foundational collection covering distributed AI, cooperation, negotiation

[3] N. R. Jennings, K. Sycara, and M. Wooldridge, "A roadmap of agent research and development," *Autonomous Agents and Multi-Agent Systems*, vol. 1, no. 1, pp. 7–38, 1998.
- Influential roadmap establishing MAS research agenda

### Coordination & Cooperation

[4] T. W. Malone and K. Crowston, "The interdisciplinary study of coordination," *ACM Computing Surveys*, vol. 26, no. 1, pp. 87–119, 1994.
- Foundational work on coordination theory applicable to agent systems

[5] E. H. Durfee, "Distributed problem solving and planning," in *Multiagent Systems: A Modern Approach to Distributed Artificial Intelligence*, G. Weiss, Ed. MIT Press, 1999, pp. 121–164.
- Distributed planning and task allocation fundamentals

[6] S. Ossowski, *Co-ordination in Artificial Agent Societies*. Berlin: Springer, 1999.
- Social coordination mechanisms for agent societies

## 2. Swarm Intelligence

### Biological Inspiration

[7] E. Bonabeau, M. Dorigo, and G. Theraulaz, *Swarm Intelligence: From Natural to Artificial Systems*. Oxford University Press, 1999.
- Foundational text on swarm intelligence principles from biology

[8] M. Dorigo and T. Stützle, *Ant Colony Optimization*. Cambridge, MA: MIT Press, 2004.
- Comprehensive treatment of ACO algorithms and applications

[9] J. Kennedy and R. Eberhart, "Particle swarm optimization," in *Proc. IEEE Int. Conf. Neural Networks*, vol. 4, 1995, pp. 1942–1948.
- Original PSO paper, foundational for swarm optimization

### Collective Intelligence

[10] P. Levy, *Collective Intelligence: Mankind's Emerging World in Cyberspace*. Cambridge, MA: Perseus Books, 1997.
- Conceptual framework for collective intelligence

[11] T. W. Malone, R. Laubacher, and C. Dellarocas, "The collective intelligence genome," *MIT Sloan Management Review*, vol. 51, no. 3, pp. 21–31, 2010.
- Taxonomy of collective intelligence systems

## 3. Software Agents & Agent-Oriented Software Engineering

### AOSE Methodologies

[12] F. Zambonelli, N. R. Jennings, and M. Wooldridge, "Developing multiagent systems: The Gaia methodology," *ACM Trans. Software Engineering and Methodology*, vol. 12, no. 3, pp. 317–370, 2003.
- Influential methodology for designing MAS

[13] P. Bresciani, A. Perini, P. Giorgini, F. Giunchiglia, and J. Mylopoulos, "Tropos: An agent-oriented software development methodology," *Autonomous Agents and Multi-Agent Systems*, vol. 8, no. 3, pp. 203–236, 2004.
- Requirements-driven agent development methodology

[14] M. Luck, P. McBurney, O. Shehory, and S. Willmott, *Agent Technology: Computing as Interaction*. AgentLink, 2005.
- AgentLink roadmap for agent technology

### Agent Communication

[15] FIPA, "FIPA ACL Message Structure Specification," Foundation for Intelligent Physical Agents, Standard SC00061G, 2002.
- Standard for agent communication language

[16] M. P. Singh, "Agent communication languages: Rethinking the principles," *IEEE Computer*, vol. 31, no. 12, pp. 40–47, 1998.
- Critical analysis of ACL design principles
