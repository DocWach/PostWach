## Pipeline states
- **Journal / conference track:** `drafting` → `internal-review` → `submitted` → `under-review` → `revision` → `accepted` → `published` | `rejected` | `dormant` | `withdrawn`
- **Workshop-abstract track (e.g. SERC AI4SE/SE4AI):** `submitted` → `accepted` → `presented`. **The abstract itself is never "published."** These entries need a *second-pass catalog* when the decision notice arrives (submitted → accepted), and again after the workshop (→ presented).
- **Research-artifact track (R016):** `(a) research artifact` items are not on a submission track; they enter the journal/conference track only when the principal targets a venue.
