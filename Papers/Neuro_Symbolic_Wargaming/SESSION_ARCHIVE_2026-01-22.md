## Session Summary

This session focused on developing comprehensive deliverables for a NATO STO Technical Activity Proposal (TAP) on integrating neuro-symbolic AI into wargaming, mission engineering, and acquisition decision-making.

## Deliverables Created

### 1. ME-Wargame Mapping Matrix
- **Files:** `ME_Wargame_Mapping_Matrix.md` (50,257 bytes), `ME_Wargame_Mapping_Matrix.html` (68,636 bytes)
- **Content:** Mapping of 42 Mission Engineering gap questions to appropriate wargame designs
- **Key Features:** 9 gap areas, wargame type taxonomy, neural/symbolic contributions, DOTMLPFI mapping

### 2. Neuro-Symbolic Architecture Specification
- **Files:** `Neuro_Symbolic_Architecture_Specification.md` (83,891 bytes), `Neuro_Symbolic_Architecture.html` (45,082 bytes)
- **Content:** Technical architecture for neuro-symbolic AI integration
- **Key Features:** Knowledge Graph Schema (OWL 2), 5 Neural components, 5 Symbolic components, OpenAPI specifications, 3-year roadmap

### 3. Methodology Handbook Outline
- **Files:** `Methodology_Handbook_Outline.md` (22,009 bytes), `Methodology_Handbook_Outline.html` (26,255 bytes)
- **Content:** Practitioner handbook structure for AI-augmented wargaming
- **Key Features:** 6 Parts, 20 Chapters, 10 Appendices, 48-hour training curriculum

### 4. Pilot Wargame Designs
- **Files:** `Pilot_Wargame_Designs.md` (50,852 bytes), `Pilot_Wargame_Designs.html` (39,086 bytes)
- **Content:** 5 detailed pilot wargame specifications
- **Pilots:**

  2. Human-Machine Teaming Simulation (AUTONOMOUS CONVOY ESCORT)
  3. Communications-Denied Operations (OPERATION SILENT THUNDER)
  4. Ethics & Authority Boundaries (OPERATION MORAL COMPASS)
  5. Failure Mode Analysis (SYSTEM STRESS TEST)

### 5. Transition Roadmap
- **Files:** `Transition_Roadmap.md` (41,825 bytes), `Transition_Roadmap.html` (70,556 bytes)
- **Content:** 5-year operationalization pathway from research to operational capability
- **Key Features:** 6 CDTs, TRL 3-8 progression, governance framework, success metrics
