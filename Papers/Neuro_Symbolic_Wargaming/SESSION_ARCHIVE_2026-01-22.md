## Key Concepts Developed

### Neuro-Symbolic Integration
- **Neural:** Pattern learning, scenario generation, prediction, adversary modeling
- **Symbolic:** Knowledge graphs, reasoning, constraints, traceability, explanation
- **Integration:** Symbol grounding, bidirectional coupling, confidence calibration

### Human-AI Teaming Model
- Cognitive role allocation across Design, Facilitation, Adjudication, Analysis, Reporting
- Trust calibration framework with appropriate reliance targets
- Workload reduction targets: 60% scenario development, 80% cross-game synthesis

### Responsible AI Framework
- 6 principles: Lawfulness, Accountability, Explainability, Reliability, Governability, Bias Mitigation
- Red lines: No IHL violations, no targeting data, no certainty claims, mandatory human oversight
