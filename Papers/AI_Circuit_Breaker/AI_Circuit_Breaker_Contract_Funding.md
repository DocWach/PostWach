# Contract-Centric Funding: Metrological Trust Underwriting for Autonomous AI

## Critical Context: SBIR/STTR Status

Congressional authorization for SBIR/STTR expired September 30, 2025. The House passed H.R. 5100 (1-year extension through Sep 2026), but it hasn't cleared the Senate yet. No new SBIR/STTR solicitations can be released until reauthorization passes.

- **Agency**: U.S. Army via armysbir.army.mil
- **Funding**: Phase I up to $150K, 3-month period of performance
- **Last cycle**: January-February 2025; next cycle pending reauthorization
- **Why**: Falls under OUSD(R&E) Critical Technology Area "Trusted AI and Autonomy." Pillars 1 (measurement theory) and 2 (enforcement architecture) map directly.

### CDAO Direct-to-Phase II - Trusted AI
- **Agency**: Office of the Secretary of Defense / Chief Digital and AI Office
- **Solicitation**: DOD_SBIR_2025_P1_C4 (CDAO component)
- **Funding**: Up to $1.2M for 18-month D2P2; $40M total SBIR allocation for GenAI
- **Why**: CDAO focuses on AI-driven decision-making, operational trust, and authority-to-operate readiness. SPC-based thresholds and metrological trust framework support ATO-ready prototypes.

### NASA SBIR/STTR - Autonomous Operations
- **Agency**: NASA (transitioning to BAA format)
- **Topics**: T6.09 "Human-Autonomous System Integration for Deep Space" and autonomous operations for ground/launch systems
- **Note**: Watch NASA 2026 Info Hub for new Appendix A once reauthorization passes.
- **Why**: Safety assurance for autonomous space operations. GUM metrology is native to NASA's engineering culture.

**STTR eligibility note**: STTR requires a small business as prime (40% of work) with university performing at least 30%. PostWach would need a partnering small business to serve as prime contractor.

## Specific Rolling BAA Targets (Open Now)

### DARPA DSO Office-Wide BAA - HR001125S0013 [TOP PICK]
- **Deadline**: Rolling until June 2, 2026
- **Funding**: Up to $2M; accelerated awards within 30 days for qualifying proposals
- **Specific thrust**: "Complex, Dynamic, and Intelligent Systems" explicitly calls for:
  - **Homeostatic mechanisms** (directly maps to Pillar 3: bio-inspired adaptive regulation)
  - **Foundations of intelligence** (maps to Pillar 1: measurement theory for AI trust)
  - **Human-AI ecosystems** (maps to Pillar 2: enforcement architecture)
  - Systems "that evolve and adapt and for which traditional reductionist, data-driven and statistical methods fail"
- **Why this is the best fit**: This BAA essentially describes the project. The DSO is explicitly interested in homeostatic mechanisms applied to intelligent systems, which is the core of Pillar 3.
- **Action**: Contact DSO program managers before submitting. Submit an Executive Summary first to gauge interest.
- **Source**: SAM.gov, DARPA DSO BAA page, DSO Thrust Areas

### DARPA CLARA (within DSO BAA)
- **Solicitation**: HR001125S0013 (falls under DSO BAA)
- **Abstracts by**: March 2, 2026
- **Full proposals**: April 10, 2026
- **Funding**: Up to $2M over 24 months (15-month Phase 1 + 9-month Phase 2)
- **Award type**: Other Transaction for prototype projects
- **Why**: Seeks "high assurance, broadly applicable AI systems of systems" through hierarchical composition of ML and reasoning components. GUM-based uncertainty quantification and SPC enforcement provide exactly the assurance framework CLARA needs.
- **Information Session**: Held February 19, 2026.

### ONR Long Range BAA - N00014-25-S-B001
- **Deadline**: Rolling until September 30, 2026
- **Relevant ONR Codes**:
  - **Code 30** - Computational Methods for Decision Making; Machine Learning, Reasoning, and Intelligence (trust quantification in AI-assisted decisions; assured AI, trustworthy ML systems)
  - **Code 31 / ONR 311** - Information Assurance & Cyber Security (trust metrics for naval C5ISR systems)
  - **Code 31 / ONR 312** - Command & Control (decision support, AI for operational planning; SPC-based enforcement supports command decision trust)
  - **Code 32 / ONR 321** - Autonomy & Unmanned Systems (autonomous system trust and assurance; bio-inspired adaptive regulation for maintaining safety)
- **Action**: Contact specific Code program officers before submitting white papers. ONR strongly encourages pre-submission discussions.

### AFOSR BAA - FA9550-25-S-0001
- **Deadline**: Rolling (accepted until BAA is superseded)
- **Funding**: Typically $100K-$300K/year for 3-5 years
- **Relevant programs within RTA2 (Information and Networks)**:
  - **"Trust and Influence"** - specifically studies trust dynamics, quantifying and modeling trust in human-machine systems (Pillar 1)
  - **"Computational Cognition and Machine Intelligence"** - principles and methodologies for intelligent machine behavior (GUM metrology + bio-inspired regulation)
  - **"Dynamic Data and Information Processing"** - adaptive information processing (Pillar 3)
  - **"Science of Information, Computation, and Fusion"** - information-theoretic foundations relevant to measurement uncertainty quantification
  - **"Complex Networks"** - network dynamics relevant to telecom/network operations domain
- **Relevant programs within RTA1 (Engineering and Complex Systems)**:
  - **"Dynamics and Control"** - control theory for complex systems (SPC-based control dynamics and circuit breaker enforcement)
- **Action**: AFOSR "highly encourages" proposers to contact Program Officers before submitting.

## OTA and Alternative Contract Vehicles

### CDAO Tradewinds Solutions Marketplace
- **Vehicle**: Other Transaction Authority (OTA)
- **Submissions**: Monthly, September 2025 through May 2026
- **Format**: 5-minute pitch video + detailed form at tradewindai.com
- **Assessment**: Expert peer panel within 30 days; solutions rated for "awardability"
- **Why**: Position the enforcement architecture (AI circuit breaker with SPC thresholds) as a deployable solution for DoD trusted AI operations.

### NSTXL S2MARTS OTA Consortium
- **Focus areas**: Microelectronics, cyber resiliency, spectrum superiority, trusted systems
- **How to join**: Complete membership application at nstxl.org
- **Why**: "Trusted systems" focus area aligns with metrological trust framework. S2MARTS regularly issues prototype project solicitations through OTA.

### MATAC OTA Consortium (New - January 2026)
- **Manager**: Advanced Technology International (ATI)
- **Sponsor**: Naval Surface Warfare Center, Port Hueneme Division (NSWC PHD)
- **Focus areas**: Advanced computing, machine learning, autonomous and uncrewed systems, integrated warfare systems
- **Why**: Telecom/network operations expertise + AI trust assurance for autonomous maritime systems.

### DHS Silicon Valley Innovation Program (SVIP)
- **Solicitation**: 70RSAT21R00000006
- **Deadline**: Rolling until May 20, 2026
- **Vehicle**: Innovation Other Transaction Solicitation
- **Why**: DHS focus on AI safety for critical infrastructure. Telecom is critical infrastructure.
- **Contact**: dhs-silicon-valley@hq.dhs.gov

### Army GUARD Prototype (Precedent / Follow-on Watch)
- **Program**: Generative Unwanted Activity Recognition and Defense (GUARD)
- **Value**: ~$6.3M OTA
- **Awarded to**: ATI / Battelle Memorial Institute
- **Purpose**: Detect unpredictable AI behavior and ensure autonomous systems are "trustworthy and effective for future military operations"
- **Why this matters**: Validates demand for exactly this type of research. Monitor ATI/NSTXL consortia for follow-on solicitations.
