# Phase 9: Several Complex Variables & Potential Theory

## Overview

Phase 9 implements the MSC2020 coverage for several complex variables and potential theory, spanning MSC categories 31 and 32.

## MSC Categories Covered

| MSC | Category | Agent(s) |
|-----|----------|----------|
| 31 | Potential theory | `potential-theorist` |
| 32 | Several complex variables | `several-complex-variables-analyst` |

## Agents (2)

### `several-complex-variables-analyst` (MSC 32)
Expert in holomorphic functions of several variables, domains of holomorphy, and ∂̄-theory.

**Key Theorems:** Hartogs Extension, Oka-Cartan Theory, Hörmander L² Estimates, Levi Problem

### `potential-theorist` (MSC 31)
Expert in harmonic functions, capacity, and the Dirichlet problem.

**Key Theorems:** Maximum Principle, Harnack's Inequality, Wiener Criterion, Riesz Decomposition

## Skills (2)

- `several-complex-variables` - Pseudoconvexity, ∂̄-problem, Stein manifolds
- `potential-theory` - Harmonic functions, capacity, Dirichlet problem
